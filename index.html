üöÄ Why Stellar?

Stellar offers:

  • Fast settlement (3-5 seconds)
  • Negligible transaction fees (~$0.00001)
  • Regulatory-compliant asset issuance
  • Support for multi-asset wallets and smart contracts (via Soroban)
